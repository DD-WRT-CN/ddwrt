

The NPort Linux driver supports Linux Kernel 2.6 
===============================================================

1. The first NPort Linux driver which supports Kernel 2.6 is Ver1.6. 

2. The driver is tested on Mandrake 10 and 
   Fedora Core2 to support Kernel 2.6.
   If any compatible issue, please contact
   support@moxa.com

3. The driver difference under 2.6 and 2.4 kernel:
   a. The cur* device is obsolete. That is,
      you can't use cum* device under kernel
      2.6.
